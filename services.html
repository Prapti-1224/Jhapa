Our Services

Tourism Information

Guides to local attractions, temples, tea gardens, rivers, and parks.Maps and travel tips for visitors.

Cultural Events & Festivals

Jhapa celebrates a rich blend of festivals, including Dashain, Tihar, Chhath, and Maghe Sankranti. Visitors can enjoy traditional dances, music, and local food, experiencing the vibrant culture of the district.

Transportation Service

Details about road networks, public transport, bus routes, and connectivity to major cities. Travel assistance and transport updates help visitors and locals move safely and easily.

Sports

Information about sports clubs, youth programs, and community training centers. Activities encourage youth engagement, leadership, and healthy lifestyles.

Health & Medical Services

Access to hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, and health campaigns in the district. Information about vaccination programs, maternal care, and emergency medical support is available for residents.
