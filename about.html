Welcome to the website of Jhapa District.

Located in the Terai region of Koshi Province. Jhapa is known for its fertile land, rich agriculture, and diverse communities. The district shares its eastern border with India and is one of the most important agricultural regions of Nepal.

Who We Are

This website aims to provide reliable information about Jhapa’s geography, culture, tourism, agriculture, and public services. Our goal is to promote awareness, development, and pride in the district. Jhapa is located in the eastern Terai of Nepal. It has fertile land, rivers, and lush green landscapes.The district shares its eastern border with India and plays a significant role in agriculture and trade.Jhapa is known for its rich natural beauty, including green fields, flowing rivers, tea gardens, and religious sites. The peaceful environment and scenic landscapes make it an attractive destination for visitors and a proud home for its residents. The district is culturally diverse, where communities such as Brahmin, Chhetri, Rai, Limbu, Tamang, Rajbanshi, and Madhesi live together in harmony. Festivals like Dashain, Tihar, and Chhath are celebrated with unity and joy, reflecting the strong cultural bond of the people.
