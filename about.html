Who We Are

Geography

Jhapa is located in the eastern Terai of Nepal. It has fertile land, rivers, and lush green landscapes.

Culture

Home to diverse communities, Jhapa celebrates multiple festivals, traditions, and local arts.

Economy

Jhapa is famous for agriculture, tea gardens, and trade with neighboring districts and India.
