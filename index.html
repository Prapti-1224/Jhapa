Welcome to Jhapa

The Land of Natural Beauty & Cultural Diversity

Get some knowledge of jhapa

Geography

Jhapa District is located in the Terai region of Koshi Province, featuring flat and fertile land. It is bordered by India to the east and Morang District to the west. Major rivers like Mechi, Kankai, and Biring flow through the district, supporting agriculture. The climate is tropical, with hot summers, monsoon rains, and mild winters.

Natural Beauty

Jhapa is known for its lush green fields, rivers, and peaceful landscapes. Sites like Arjundhara add scenic and spiritual charm. Tea gardens and forests nearby enhance its serene and refreshing environment. Sunrise and sunset views over the plains create picturesque scenery.

Culture

Jhapa is culturally diverse, home to ethnic groups such as Brahmin, Chhetri, Rai, Limbu, Tamang, Newar, Rajbanshi, and Madhesi. Festivals like Dashain, Tihar, Chhath, and Maghe Sankranti are celebrated with joy. Traditional music, dances, and local foods reflect its rich heritage. The people are known for hospitality, unity, and hardworking nature.
