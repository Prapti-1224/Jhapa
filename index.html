About Jhapa

Geography

Jhapa lies in the southeastern part of Nepal, known for fertile plains and favorable climate.

Culture

The district is home to diverse communities with rich cultural traditions and festivals.

Tourism

Tea gardens, Kankai Bridge, Domukha, Gauradaha and many natural attractions make Jhapa a tourism hub.

Population

Jhapa has a diverse population with a mix of ethnic groups living harmoniously.
